Cloud Study Jam

  • Gajesh Naik
  • GDG Goa

GDG Goa had organized a workshop on Cloud Study Jam at 91Springboard Panaji Goa. I wanted to get started on the Google Cloud in more details. Hence I joined for Cloud Study Jam which offered official Google training, worth $55+ value, free of charge. It offered me to work through several Google Cloud labs. I got hands-on experience with the Cloud Console, IoT on Cloud, and Machine Learning. After live session, I got free access to more labs as homework. After completing all labs in the quest, I earned a Google-hosted badge for my online profile, and additional 30 days access to the training platform to complete any labs of my wish. There was a brief quiz to recollect the names of Google Products and Services. I enlisted maximum 20 and was thus offered Google Study Jam Badge as token of appreciation.
